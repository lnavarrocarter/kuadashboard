IMPORTANT — macOS Gatekeeper Notice
=====================================

KuaDashboard is not signed with an Apple Developer certificate.
macOS will block the first launch. To allow it:

1. Open the DMG and drag KuaDashboard to Applications as usual.
2. Try to open KuaDashboard — macOS will show a warning.
3. Go to System Settings > Privacy & Security.
4. Scroll down — you will see a message about KuaDashboard being blocked.
5. Click "Open Anyway" and confirm.

Alternatively, run this command in Terminal:
  xattr -cr /Applications/KuaDashboard.app

The app only needs this step once. After that it will open normally.

By clicking "Agree" you acknowledge this notice and accept the terms of use.
